<Title>Post Office—postal Delays</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 11 August 1885 vol 300 c1730</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-11</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>89</WordCount>


<speaker>COLONEL NOLAN</speaker> asked the Postmaster General, If his attention has been drawn to the fact that it takes two days to forward a letter from Whitegate to Woodford, about eight miles distant; and if he would take some step to facilitate postal communication between Whitegate and the remainder of the county? 
<speaker>THE POSTMASTER GENERAL (Lord JOHN MANNERS)</speaker> I am much obliged to the hon. and gallant Member for calling attention to this matter. I have directed inquiries to be made, with a view of remedying the inconvenience complained of.
