<Title>Public Health (Metropolis)—contamination of the River Lea</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 04 August 1885 vol 300 c1064</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-04</SittingDate>

In reply to Mr. JOHN HOLMS, 
<speaker>THE PRESIDENT of the LOCAL GOVERNMENT BOARD (Mr. A. J. BALFOUR)</speaker> said, that the Local Authority of Tottenham had decided to construct sewage works, with the view of removing the sewage of the district from the River Lea; and the Local Government Board had sanctioned the requisite loan.
