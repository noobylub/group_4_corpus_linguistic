<Title>Egypt (The Soudan)—reported Death of Osman Digna</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 04 August 1885 vol 300 c1064</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-04</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>47</WordCount>

In answer to Sir WALTER B. BAKTTELOT, 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY OF STATE (Mr. W. H. SMITH)</speaker> said, that a telegram had been received to the effect that Osman Digna was believed to have been killed, but a subsequent telegram stated that the report of his death was untrue.
