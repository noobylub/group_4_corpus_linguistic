<speaker>MR. WARTON</speaker> asked the Secretary to the Board of Trade, Whether the Government intend to take into consideration the question of amending "The Electric Lighting Act, 1882 ?" 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY (Baron HENRY DE WORMS)</speaker> Her Majesty's Government do not propose to undertake the consideration of this question during the present Session.
