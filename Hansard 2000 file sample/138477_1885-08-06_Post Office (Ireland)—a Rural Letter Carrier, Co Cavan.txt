<Title>Post Office (Ireland)—a Rural Letter Carrier, Co Cavan</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 06 August 1885 vol 300 cc1266-7</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-06</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>66</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. BIGGAR</speaker> asked the Postmaster General, Whether Andrew Norris, rural letter carrier for the Shercock and Knappa District of county Cavan, absented himself from his duties on the 13th July; and, if so, did he have leave of absence from his postmaster? 
<speaker>THE POSTMASTER GENERAL (Lord JOHN MANNERS)</speaker> I am informed that on the 13th of July the letter carrier in question was absent on leave.
