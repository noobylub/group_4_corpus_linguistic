<Title>Egypt (The Soudan)—military Expedition—the Coldstream Guards</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 19 May 1885 vol 298 c947</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-05-19</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>48</WordCount>


<speaker>VISCOUNT LEWISHAM</speaker> asked the Secretary of State for War, If the Cold-stream Guards have sailed from Suakin; if so, whether they are on board the Deccan or the Jumna? 
<speaker>SIR ARTHUR HAYTER</speaker> (who replied) said: The Cold stream Guards left Suakin on the 17th instant in the Deccan.
