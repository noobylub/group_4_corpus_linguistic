<speaker>SIR ROBERT FOWLER (LORD MAYOR)</speaker> asked Mr. Chancellor of the Exchequer, Whether the Government had received any confirmation of the reported death of the Mahdi? 
<speaker>THE CHANCELLOR or THE EXCHEQUER</speaker> No, Sir; we have received no official confirmation of the report.
