<Title>Post Office—mails to the Western Islands</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 03 August 1885 vol 300 c837</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-03</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>45</WordCount>


<speaker>LORD COLIN CAMPBELL</speaker> asked the Postmaster General, Whether any, and, if any, what arrangement has been made to accelerate the Mails to the Western Islands? 
<speaker>THE POSTMASTER GENERAL (Lord JOHN MANNERS),</speaker> in reply, said, that the subject was now under the consideration of the Treasury.
