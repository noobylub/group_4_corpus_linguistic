Order for Second Reading read. 
<speaker>SIR FARRER HERSCHELL</speaker> said, he should like to know what the Bill was? 
<speaker>THE PRESIDENT OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT BOARD (Mr. A. J. BALFOUR)</speaker> said, it was to enable the masters of workhouses to take in lunatics, who had been wandering about the streets, for a short time. Bill read a second time, and committed for Tomorrow.
