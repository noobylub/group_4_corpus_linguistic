<Title>Supply—grant to hrh Princess Beatrice</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 05 June 1885 vol 298 c1343</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-06-05</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>99</WordCount>


<speaker>SIR GEORGE CAMPBELL</speaker> said, he had seen on the Paper today for the first time a Notice with regard to a grant of £30,000 to Princess Beatrice as a marriage portion. He wished to ask the Chancellor of the Exchequer whether he would promise not to bring on that subject tonight at an unusually late hour? 
<speaker>THE CHANCELLOR OF THE EXCHEQUER (Mr. CHILDERS)</speaker> That is the customary Resolution following upon the grant of £6,000 a-year to Her Royal Highness. It has been put down for Committee tonight, and I trust there will be no objection to proceeding with it.
