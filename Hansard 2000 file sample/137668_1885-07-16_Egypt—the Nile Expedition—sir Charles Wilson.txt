<Title>Egypt—the Nile Expedition—sir Charles Wilson</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 16 July 1885 vol 299 cc914-5</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-07-16</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>80</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. ARTHUR O'CONNOR</speaker> asked the Secretary of State for War, Whether he will obtain from Lord Wolseley the terms of his request for explanation of Sir Charles Wilson's delay, and lay the same upon the Table? 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR WAR (Mr. W. H. SMITH)</speaker> I do not propose to ask for this letter from Lord Wolseley. It does not appear to me that it would add anything to the information which the public already has on this matter.
