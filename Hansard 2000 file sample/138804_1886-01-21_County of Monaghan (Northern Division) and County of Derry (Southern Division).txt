<Title>County of Monaghan (Northern Division) and County of Derry (Southern Division)</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 21 January 1886 vol 302 c85</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1886-01-21</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>90</WordCount>

Mr. SPEAKER acquainted the House that ho had received a Letter from Timothy M. Healy, esquire, returned as a Member for the County of Monaghan (Northern Division), and for the County of Derry (Southern Division), making his election to serve for South Derry; and the said Letter was read as followeth:— 21 Jan. 1886. Sir, Having been returned for North Monaghan and South Derry, I beg to say that I elect to sit for South Derry. I am Sir, Your obedient Servant, The Right Honble. T. M. HEALY. The Speaker.
