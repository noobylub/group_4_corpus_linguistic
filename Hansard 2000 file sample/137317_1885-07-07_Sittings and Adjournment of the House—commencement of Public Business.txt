<Title>Sittings and Adjournment of the House—commencement of Public Business</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 07 July 1885 vol 298 c1824</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-07-07</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>44</WordCount>


<speaker>THE CHANCELLOR OF THE EXCHEQUER</speaker> said, perhaps it might be the pleasure of the House that after today the Public Business of the House should commence at a quarter-past 4. He understood that Private Business was sufficiently advanced to admit of that being done.
