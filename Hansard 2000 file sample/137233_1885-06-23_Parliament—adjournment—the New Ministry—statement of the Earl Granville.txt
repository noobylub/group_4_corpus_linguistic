<Title>Parliament—adjournment—the New Ministry—statement of the Earl Granville</Title>
<DebRef>HL Deb 23 June 1885 vol 298 c1607</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-06-23</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Lords</SittingType>
<WordCount>45</WordCount>


<speaker>EARL GRANVILLE</speaker> My Lords, I am authorized to state that Lord Salisbury has gone down to Windsor, and has accepted Office; and at his desire I propose, when the proceedings of the day close, to move the adjournment of the House, as usual, till Thursday.
