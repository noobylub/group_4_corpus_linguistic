<speaker>MR. MACFARLANE</speaker> said, ho saw by the Orders that the Crofters' Bill was marked with the asterisk as if it were a Government Order. He wished to ask the Chancellor of the Exchequer if it was intended to proceed with it? 
<speaker>THE CHANCELLOR OF THE EXCHEQUER</speaker> That is a mistake; it ought not to be "starred." DR. CAMERON said, that, as there was no block against this Bill, if no one else did, he would move the second reading of the Bill when the Order was reached. MR. J. LOWTHER In the event of the second reading being moved, I shall move that the Order be discharged.
