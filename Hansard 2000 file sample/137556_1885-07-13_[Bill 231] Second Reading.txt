Order for Second Beading read. 
<speaker>COLONEL NOLAN</speaker> said, this was exactly the same Bill as the Act of last year. Its object was to continue the Act up to May next, and it was proposed on account of the danger of cholera reaching their shores from the Continent. Bill read a second time, and committed for Tomorrow.
