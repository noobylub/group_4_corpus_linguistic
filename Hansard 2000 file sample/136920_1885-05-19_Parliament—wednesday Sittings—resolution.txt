<Title>Parliament—wednesday Sittings—resolution</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 19 May 1885 vol 298 c950</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-05-19</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>80</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. WARTON</speaker> rose to call attention to the subject of Wednesday Sittings; and to move— That on a Wednesday, if a House be not made by a quarter to One of the clock, Mr. Speaker do adjourn the House without Motion made or Question put; that the said Resolution be a Standing Order of this House. Notice taken, that 40 Members wore not present; House counted, and 40 Members not being present, House adjourned at a quarter after Six o'clock.
