<Title>Post Office—registration of Telegraphic Addresses</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 07 August 1885 vol 300 c1459</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-07</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>55</WordCount>


<speaker>COLONEL KING-HARMAN</speaker> asked the Postmaster General, Whether, under the Telegraph Acts Amendment Bill , any alteration would be made in the existing system of registration of addresses for telegraphic purposes? 
<speaker>THE POSTMASTER GENERAL (Lord JOHN MANNERS)</speaker> It is not proposed to make any alteration in the charge or the system for the registration of addresses.
