<Title>Post Office (Ireland)—west of Ireland Mail Service</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 13 August 1885 vol 301 c14</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-13</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>85</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. SEXTON</speaker> asked the Financial Secretary to the Treasury, Whether the arrangement for the improved mail service between Dublin and the West of Ireland has been completed; and, if so, on or about what date the new service may be expected to begin? 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY TO THE TREASURY (Sir HENRY HOLLAND)</speaker> I think I can safely say that a new contract for the conveyance of the mails on the Midland Great Western Railway of Ireland will come into force on the 1st of October next.
