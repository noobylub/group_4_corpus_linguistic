<Title>Medical Act Amendment Bill</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 21 May 1885 vol 298 c1038</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-05-21</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>97</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. ARTHUR O'CONNOR</speaker> I wish to ask the Vice President of the Council, Whether his attention has been directed to a statement in The Medical Press of the 20th instant, stating that the Government intend to introduce the Medical Act Amendment Bill of last Session; and, whether, if that is the case, any clause or clauses, as Amendments, can be placed upon the Paper? 
<speaker>MR. MUNDELLA</speaker> I have not seen The Medical, Press; and I have not had any intimation that it is the intention of the Government to introduce the Medical Act Amendment Bill this Session.
