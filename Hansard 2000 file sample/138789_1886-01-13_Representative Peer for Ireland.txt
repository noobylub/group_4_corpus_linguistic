Writs and Returns electing the Viscount Bangor a Representative Peer for Ireland in the room of the late Earl of Erne, deceased, with the Certificate of the Clerk of the Crown in Ireland annexed thereto: Delivered (on oath), and Certificate read. Several Lords—took the Oath. House adjourned at Four o'clock, till Tomorrow, Two o'clock.
