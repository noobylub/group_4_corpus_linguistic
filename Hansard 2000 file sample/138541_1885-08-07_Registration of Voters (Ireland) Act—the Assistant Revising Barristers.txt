<Title>Registration of Voters (Ireland) Act—the Assistant Revising Barristers</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 07 August 1885 vol 300 c1457</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-07</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>68</WordCount>

MR. O'BRIEN asked the Chief Secretary to the Lord Lieutenant of Ireland, Whether he is yet in a position to name the Assistant Revising Barristers about to be appointed under the new Registration Act in Ireland? 
<speaker>THE CHIEF SECRETARY (Sir WILLIAM HART DYKE)</speaker> If the hon. Member will put this Question to me on Tuesday next I shall probably be in a position to give him the information.
