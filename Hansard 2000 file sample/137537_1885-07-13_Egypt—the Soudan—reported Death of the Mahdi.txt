<Title>Egypt—the Soudan—reported Death of the Mahdi</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 13 July 1885 vol 299 c436</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-07-13</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>60</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. BRODRICK</speaker> I wish to ask the Under Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Whether he has any official information of the reported death of the Mahdi? 
<speaker>THE UNDER SECRETARY OF STATE (Mr. BOURKE)</speaker> said, the Government had received from official sources information to the same effect as that which was given in the newspapers; but there was nothing definite.
