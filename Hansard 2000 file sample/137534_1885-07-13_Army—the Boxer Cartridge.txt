<Title>Army—the Boxer Cartridge</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 13 July 1885 vol 299 cc434-5</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-07-13</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>95</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. JERNINGHAM</speaker> asked the Secretary of State for War, Whether he will lay upon the Table of the House a Statement addressed to him by General E. M. Boxer on the subject of the alleged failure of the Boxer Cartridge in the late operations in the Soudan? 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY OF STATE (Mr. W. H. SMITH)</speaker> The transactions to which this Memorandum refers occurred 15 years ago. There is much controversial matter in the statement; and as the ordinary means of publication are open to General Boxer, I must decline to present it officially to Parliament.
