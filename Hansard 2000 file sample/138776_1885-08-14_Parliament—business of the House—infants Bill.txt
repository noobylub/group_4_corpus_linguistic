<Title>Parliament—business of the House—infants Bill</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 14 August 1885 vol 301 c37</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-14</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>58</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. FIRTH</speaker> asked whether the House could not proceed with the Infants Bill , as it was down on the Orders of the Day? 
<speaker>MR. SPEAKER</speaker> , in reply, said, the Orders of the Day had not been reached. There could be no object in entering upon the Orders of the Day with the prospect of immediate interruption.
