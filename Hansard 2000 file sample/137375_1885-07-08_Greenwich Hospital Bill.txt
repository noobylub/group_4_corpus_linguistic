On Motion of Mr. ASHMEAD-BARTLETT, Bill to provide for defraying the Expenditure on account of Greenwich Hospital directly out of the Revenues of Greenwich Hospital; to amend in other respects the Greenwich Hospital Acts, 1860 to 1883; and for other purposes relating thereto, ordered to be brought in by Mr. ASHMEAD-BARTLETT and Sir HENRY HOLLAND.
