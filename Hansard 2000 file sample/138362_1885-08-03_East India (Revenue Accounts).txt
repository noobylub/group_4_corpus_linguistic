<Title>East India (Revenue Accounts)</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 03 August 1885 vol 300 cc849-50</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-03</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>50</WordCount>

Ordered, That the several Accounts and Papers which have been presented to the House in this Session of Parliament, relating to the Revenues of India, be referred to the consideration of a Committee of the whole House." Resolved, That this House will, upon Thursday, resolve itself into the said Committee.
