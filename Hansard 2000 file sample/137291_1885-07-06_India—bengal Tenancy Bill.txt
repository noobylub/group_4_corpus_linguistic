<Title>India—bengal Tenancy Bill</Title>

<WordCount>84</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. BAXTER</speaker> asked the Secretary of State for India, Whether the Bengal Tenancy Bill has received the assent of the Secretary of State in Council? 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR INDIA (Lord RANDOLPH CHURCHILL)</speaker> The Government of India were informed by my Predecessor, in a despatch dated the 23rd June last, that the Bengal Tenancy Act would be left to its operation. The despatch conveying this intimation will be included in a collection of Papers relating to the subject, now being prepared for presentation.
