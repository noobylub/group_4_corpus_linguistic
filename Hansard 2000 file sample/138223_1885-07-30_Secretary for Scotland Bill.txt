<Title>Secretary for Scotland Bill</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 30 July 1885 vol 300 c522</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-07-30</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>87</WordCount>


<speaker>MR. BUCHANAN</speaker> asked the Secretary of State for the Home Department, Whether it is the intention of Her Majesty's Government, under the Secretary for Scotland Bill , to transfer the permanent staff of the Education Office, who at present transact the business of Scottish Education, to the office of the new Minister? 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY OF STATE (Sir R. ASSHETON CROSS)</speaker> I think it is rather premature to give any opinion on this subject until we have heard the discussion on the Bill on the question of education.
