<Title>Parliament—business of the House</Title>

<SittingDate>1885-05-21</SittingDate>

<WordCount>61</WordCount>

MR. GLADSTONE said, it might be convenient for the House to know that it was proposed to take on Friday, first, the second reading of the Telegraph Acts Amendment Bill ; next, the second reading of the Criminal Law Amendment Bill , which had come down from the House of Lords; and, thirdly, the Ulster Canal and Tyrone Navigation Bill .
