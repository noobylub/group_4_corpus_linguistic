Ordered, That the Select Committee on Postal Service (Great Britain and North America) do consist of Seventeen Members:— Committee nominated of, — Mr. HENRY LEE, Mr. NORTHCOTE, Mr. SHAW LEFEVRE, Mr. WILLIAM LOWTHER, Mr. SEXTON, Mr. BRODRICK, Mr. DEASY, Mr. WILLIAMSON, Sir HENRY SELWIN-IBBETSON, Mr. R.
