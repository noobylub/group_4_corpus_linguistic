Bill considered in Committee. (In the Committee.) Clauses 1 to 3, inclusive, agreed to. Clause 4 (Specifications, &c. not to be published unless application accepted). 
<speaker>MR. TOMLINSON</speaker> said, he had no observation to make as to the clause; but he thought Progress should be reported before the clause was agreed to, because he expected that he or some other Member might have an additional clause to move. 
<speaker>SIR FARBER HERSCHELL</speaker> said, he had no objection to reporting Progress. Clause agreed to. Committee report Progress; to sit again Tomorrow.
