<Title>Parliament—business of the House —Secretary for Scotland Bill</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 03 August 1885 vol 300 c849</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-08-03</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>63</WordCount>


<speaker>SIR LYON PLAYFAIR</speaker> said, that in view of the assurance formerly given, that they would receive proper time for the discussion of the Secretary for Scotland Bill , he would ask Mr. Chancellor of the Exchequer after what hour he would not take that Bill that night? 
<speaker>THE CHANCELLOR OF THE EXCHEQUER</speaker> I am afraid that is a Question I can hardly answer.
