<Title>Navy—the Pay of Hobart Pasha</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 09 July 1885 vol 299 c126</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-07-09</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>
<WordCount>53</WordCount>

In reply to Sir GEORGE CAMPBELL, 
<speaker>THE FIRST LORD OP THE ADMI RALTY (Lord GEORGE HAMILTON)</speaker> said, that there was no probability of the introduction of a Supplementary Estimate to provide for the pay of Admiral Hobart-Hampden. He thought that such a small sum could be obtained out of the Supplies already granted.
