<Title>Fishery Piers and Harbours (Ireland—the Greystones Harbour</Title>
<DebRef>HC Deb 23 July 1885 vol 299 c1624</DebRef>
<SittingDate>1885-07-23</SittingDate>
<SittingType>Commons</SittingType>

<speaker>MR. W. J. CORBET</speaker> asked the Financial Secretary to the Treasury, If Grey-stones Harbour is to be constructed on the monolithic system, as asked by the inhabitants of the town in a recent Memorial to the Treasury? 
<speaker>THE SECRETARY TO THE TREASURY (Sir HENRY HOLLAND)</speaker> I am making inquiries into this matter, and I would be obliged to the hon. Member if he would postpone his Question until Monday.
