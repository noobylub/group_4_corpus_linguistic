Order for Consideration, as amended, read. Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill, as amended, be now-considered."—( Sir Charles W. Dilke. ) 
<speaker>COLONEL MAKINS</speaker> desired to make an Amendment to Clause 6, by leaving out seven days, and inserting 48 hours. 
<speaker>SIR CHARLES W. DILKE</speaker> said, there was a general agreement on this point, and he did not think they could accept the Amendment. 
<speaker>COLONEL MAKINS</speaker> Then I must object to the Bill being taken. 
<speaker>SIR CHARLES W. DILKE</speaker> We must put it off then. Consideration, as amended, deferred till Tomorrow.
